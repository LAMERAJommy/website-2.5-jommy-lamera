Tips & Tricks to Improve Your Network

Here are some simple yet effective ways to enhance your internet speed and stability:

  • Restart your router regularly to clear cached data.
  • Place your router in a central location for better Wi-Fi coverage.
  • Use a wired connection (Ethernet) for more stable speeds.
  • Reduce interference by keeping your router away from other electronic devices.
  • Update your router's firmware to ensure optimal performance.
  • Secure your network with a strong password to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Limit the number of connected devices to reduce bandwidth congestion.
  • Use Quality of Service (QoS) settings to prioritize essential applications.
